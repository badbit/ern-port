        I have heard the question many times, "How do I know when I am a hacker?". Each time that I heard it, I would ponder it for days at a time, thinking, how did i know when i was a hacker. It has taken me a long time to discover what makes a person a hacker, and what traits a true hacker has. I decided to write this file in order to help newcomers know when they have become hackers, and to provoke the thought of this question in the minds of the advanced hackers.
