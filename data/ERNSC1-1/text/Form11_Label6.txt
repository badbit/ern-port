        One day I recieved a few questions from some beginning hackers that I met on IRC (Internet Relay Chat). I proudly answered their questions, and I began thinking back to when I was a beginner. I remembered how difficult it was to gather all of the information I wanted. So I decided to write a beginners guide called "The Ultimate Beginner's Guide To Hacking And Phreaking". It is available now on various hacking W.W.W. Pages. These pages are listed at the end of this file. I sent it out to my friends and they in turn posted it on their pages.
